Recipe for Simplicity

The website's purpose

Recipe for Simplicity is a cooking website where I show to you some of my favorite recipes that are easy to make, even for people who have never cooked in their entire lives! This website will include a list of stews, fried dishes, and the lot that will impress your friends and family!

All about Max

Max is a good cook who wants to share his recipes with people all over the world! When he was a kid, his dream was to be a chef and prepare food that will warm the soul. Ever since the pandemic, he was making recepies for most of his dishes so he can share them with you!
