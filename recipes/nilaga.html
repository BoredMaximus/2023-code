Recipe for Simplicity

Nilaga

Nilaga is a simple beef soup that is great for those cold windy days. Soft potatoes, crunchy bok choy and that hearty broth can warm those cold hardened souls.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef shanks and/or brisket
  • 3 potatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 bok choy
  • 2 corn cobs, roughly chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce

Steps:

  1. On medium heat, saute your onion until it becomes soft. Then, add in your beef and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Pour in the water and let it boil. Then, add your peppercorns and fish sauce and lower your heat to low to simmer for 1-2 hours.
  3. Put-in the vegetables starting with the potato and the corn. Simmer for 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Add the bok choy (Pechay). Cover the pot and turn off the heat. Let it stay for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
