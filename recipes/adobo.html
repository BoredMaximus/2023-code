Sweet Pork Adobo

The humble sweet pork adobo, one of my favorite dishes when I was a child. Sweet, savory and meaty, this dish brings comfort to a loving home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 5 Cloves of Garlic, diced
  • 3 Potatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 kg Pork, sliced into small chunks
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Vinegar
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4 tbsp Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In medium-high heat, Dice your onions and garlic and saute them in a pot, then chop your pork and saute them as well. It should release a nice aroma and the pork should have a little bit of browning in it.
  2. Add water, soy sauce and vinegar to the pot until the pork is atleast submerged, then add your bay leaves and lower the heat to medium low and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Chop your potatoes and add it to the pot and let it simmer for 15 more minutes. If you get a piece a pork with a fork and it's soft, add brown sugar, salt and pepper.
  4. Serve on a plate with rice and enjoy!
