Recipe for Simplicity

Chicken Tinola

Chicken Tinola, a relatively simple dish, meant to warm the soul with it's soft, fall off the bone chicken and hearty broth. It will make those windy days a bit more bearable.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kilo whole chicken thighs/wings
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, diced
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 knob of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 1 bunch of moringa leaves, stems trimmed
  • 2 large sayote, roughly chopped

Steps:

  1. On medium-low heat, saute onion and garlic until it becomes fragrant. Then sear chicken thighs/wings until slightly browned.
  2. Pour water into pot, then add ginger, fish sauce and sayote. Lower the heat and simmer for atleast 45 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat and add moringa leaves into the pot.
  4. Serve into bowls with rice and enjoy!
