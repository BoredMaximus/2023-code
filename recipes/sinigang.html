Recipe for Simplicity

Pork Singang

Pork Sinigang, a filipino dish that is interesting in its flavor profile. Using tamarind to make its sour taste, this dish will spice up your dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg pork, chopped into bite sized pieces
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 knob of ginger, peeled and diced
  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
  • 4 cups of water

Steps:

  1. On medium-high heat, saute the onion until it becomes fragrant then sear the pork until its lightly browned.
  2. Pour in the water, fish sauce, ginger and tomatoes. Lower the heat the simmer for 40 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat and add your spinach.
  4. Serve into bowls with rice and enjoy!
