Autobiography of My Virtual Insanity

Welcome to the Autobiography of My Virtual Insanity. There, you will see the forth the life and times of me, Sagay.
We will see forth inside of my mind to see my memories and my actions.

All about me:

My name is Sagay, I'm 29 years old and I live on the island of Budud. I tend to the animals and raise crops for my fellow Oddballs and it brings me joy whenever I was able to get a good harvest of Fleets for my community. I have several friends, some of which work the same job as me, especially Jacobine, whom I always look forward to seeing everyday. I was a honest and hardworking man who sees the beauty in its simplicity. However, everything changed when something crashed into my yard and ever since then, I drove into a rabbit hole so deep, I could never get out.
